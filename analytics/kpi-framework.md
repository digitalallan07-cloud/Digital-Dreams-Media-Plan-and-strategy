# KPI Framework

## North Star Metric

**Revenue Generated from Digital Channels**

All other metrics ladder up to this primary business outcome.

## KPI Hierarchy

### Tier 1: Business Metrics (Monthly Review)

| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Revenue from digital | $10,000+/month by Q2 | CRM / Payment platform |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | < $50 | Ad spend / New customers |
| Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) | > $500 | CRM |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | > 3:1 | Calculated |
| Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) | +10% MoM | Payment platform |

### Tier 2: Marketing Performance (Weekly Review)

| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) | 200+/month | CRM |
| MQL to SQL Conversion Rate | > 25% | CRM |
| Cost Per Lead (CPL) | < $20 | Ad platforms |
| Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) | > 4:1 | Ad platforms + CRM |
| Email Subscriber Growth | +500/month | Email platform |

### Tier 3: Channel Metrics (Weekly Review)

#### Website & SEO
| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Organic Traffic | +15% MoM | Google Analytics |
| Keyword Rankings (Top 10) | 50+ keywords | Ahrefs |
| Domain Authority | 40+ (12 months) | Ahrefs |
| Bounce Rate | < 45% | Google Analytics |
| Avg. Session Duration | > 3 minutes | Google Analytics |
| Pages Per Session | > 2.5 | Google Analytics |

#### Social Media
| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Total Followers (all platforms) | 100,000 (12mo) | Native analytics |
| Engagement Rate | > 4% avg | Native analytics |
| Social Traffic to Website | 20% of total | Google Analytics |
| Share of Voice | Top 5 in niche | Social listening tool |

#### Email Marketing
| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Open Rate | > 25% | Email platform |
| Click-Through Rate | > 3.5% | Email platform |
| Unsubscribe Rate | < 0.5% | Email platform |
| Revenue Per Email | > $0.50 | Email + CRM |

#### Paid Advertising
| KPI | Target | Data Source |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | > 2% | Ad platforms |
| Cost Per Click (CPC) | < $2.00 | Ad platforms |
| Conversion Rate | > 5% | Ad platforms + GA |
| Quality Score (Google) | > 7/10 | Google Ads |

## Reporting Cadence

### Daily Monitoring
- Ad spend pacing
- Website uptime
- Social media mentions and alerts

### Weekly Report
- Channel performance summary
- Budget pacing vs. plan
- Top-performing content
- Action items for optimization

### Monthly Report
- Full KPI dashboard review
- Month-over-month comparisons
- Campaign performance analysis
- Budget utilization report
- Recommendations for next month

### Quarterly Business Review
- Progress against annual goals
- ROI analysis by channel
- Competitive landscape update
- Strategy adjustments
- Budget reallocation proposals

## Dashboard Structure

### Executive Dashboard
- Revenue from digital channels
- Total leads generated
- CAC and LTV trends
- Overall ROAS
- Top 3 wins and challenges

### Marketing Operations Dashboard
- Channel-by-channel performance
- Campaign performance comparison
- Content performance rankings
- Funnel conversion rates
- A/B test results

### Content Performance Dashboard
- Top posts by engagement
- Traffic by content type
- SEO keyword movement
- Content production velocity
- Content ROI by format

## Attribution Model

**Primary:** Data-driven attribution (Google Analytics 4)
**Secondary:** Last-click attribution for direct response campaigns

### Attribution Windows
- Display/Social: 30-day click, 7-day view
- Search: 30-day click
- Email: 7-day click
- Direct: Session-based
