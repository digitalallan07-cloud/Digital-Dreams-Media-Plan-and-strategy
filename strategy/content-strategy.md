# Content Strategy

## Content Mission

Empower businesses and creative professionals with actionable insights, tutorials, and inspiration to succeed in the digital landscape.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Educational Content (40%)
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Industry best practices
- Tool reviews and comparisons
- Case studies with real results

### 2. Thought Leadership (25%)
- Industry trend analysis
- Expert interviews and features
- Opinion pieces on digital marketing evolution
- Research reports and data-driven insights

### 3. Brand Storytelling (20%)
- Client success stories
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Team spotlights and culture
- Company milestones and updates

### 4. Community and Engagement (15%)
- User-generated content campaigns
- Q&A sessions and AMAs
- Polls, quizzes, and interactive content
- Collaboration features with audience members

## Content Formats

| Format | Frequency | Primary Channel | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|----------------|---------|
| Blog Posts | 2-3/week | Website | SEO, education |
| Short-form Video | 5/week | TikTok, Reels | Awareness, engagement |
| Long-form Video | 1/week | YouTube | Education, authority |
| Podcast Episodes | 1/week | Spotify, Apple | Thought leadership |
| Infographics | 2/month | Pinterest, LinkedIn | Shareability |
| Email Newsletter | 2/week | Email | Nurture, retention |
| Whitepapers/eBooks | 1/quarter | Website (gated) | Lead generation |

## Content Calendar Framework

### Monthly Planning Cycle

1. **Week 1:** Keyword research, topic ideation, editorial meeting
2. **Week 2-3:** Content creation, design, and review
3. **Week 4:** Scheduling, publishing, and promotion

### Seasonal and Campaign Themes

| Quarter | Theme | Key Campaigns |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| Q1 | New Year, Fresh Start | "Digital Transformation" series |
| Q2 | Growth Season | "Scale Your Business" campaign |
| Q3 | Back to Business | "Mastering Digital" workshop series |
| Q4 | Year in Review | "Future of Digital" predictions |

## Content Distribution

### Owned Channels
- Company website and blog
- Email subscriber list
- Mobile app (if applicable)

### Earned Channels
- Guest posts on industry publications
- PR and media coverage
- Organic social shares and mentions

### Paid Channels
- Boosted social posts
- Content promotion via Taboola/Outbrain
- Sponsored newsletter placements

## SEO Content Guidelines

- Target 1 primary keyword and 2-3 secondary keywords per post
- Minimum word count: 1,500 words for blog posts
- Include internal links to 3-5 related articles
- Add alt text to all images
- Use header hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- Include a clear call-to-action in every piece
