# Social Media Strategy

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram

**Goal:** Build a visually compelling brand presence and drive engagement

- **Content Mix:** 40% educational carousels, 30% Reels, 20% Stories, 10% static posts
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 feed post/day, 3-5 Stories/day, 3 Reels/week
- **Hashtag Strategy:** 20-25 hashtags per post (mix of branded, industry, and niche)
- **Key Features:** Shopping tags, collab posts, interactive Story stickers
- **Tone:** Inspirational, visual, approachable

### LinkedIn

**Goal:** Establish thought leadership and generate B2B leads

- **Content Mix:** 40% industry insights, 30% company updates, 20% employee advocacy, 10% job postings
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 post/day on weekdays
- **Engagement Strategy:** Comment on industry posts, participate in groups, publish articles
- **Key Features:** LinkedIn Articles, Newsletters, Live events
- **Tone:** Professional, authoritative, data-driven

### TikTok

**Goal:** Reach younger audiences and build brand awareness through trending content

- **Content Mix:** 50% educational tips, 30% trends/challenges, 20% behind-the-scenes
- **Posting Frequency:** 1-2 videos/day
- **Strategy:** Hook in first 3 seconds, trending sounds, storytelling format
- **Key Features:** Duets, Stitches, LIVE sessions
- **Tone:** Casual, fun, authentic

### YouTube

**Goal:** Build a library of evergreen educational content

- **Content Mix:** 50% tutorials, 25% case studies, 15% vlogs, 10% shorts
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 long-form video/week, 3 Shorts/week
- **SEO:** Keyword-optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnails
- **Key Features:** Community tab, playlists, end screens, cards
- **Tone:** Educational, detailed, conversational

### Twitter/X

**Goal:** Real-time engagement and industry networking

- **Content Mix:** 40% insights/tips, 30% engagement/replies, 20% threads, 10% promotions
- **Posting Frequency:** 3-5 tweets/day
- **Strategy:** Participate in trending conversations, Twitter Spaces, quote tweets
- **Tone:** Witty, concise, conversational

## Community Management

### Response Times
- **DMs:** Within 2 hours during business hours
- **Comments:** Within 4 hours
- **Mentions/Tags:** Within 6 hours
- **Reviews:** Within 24 hours

### Engagement Protocol
1. Respond to every comment (not just questions)
2. Like and acknowledge user-generated content
3. Share and credit community content
4. Proactively engage with followers' content
5. Address negative feedback privately when possible

## Growth Tactics

- **Cross-Promotion:** Share content across platforms with native formatting
- **Collaborations:** Partner with complementary brands for joint content
- **Contests/Giveaways:** Monthly giveaway campaigns to boost followers
- **Employee Advocacy:** Team members share company content with personal commentary
- **Hashtag Campaigns:** Branded hashtag challenges quarterly
