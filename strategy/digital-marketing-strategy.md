# Digital Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

Digital Dreams Media's digital marketing strategy focuses on building brand awareness, driving qualified traffic, and converting audiences into loyal customers through a multi-channel approach.

## Goals

1. **Brand Awareness** - Increase brand visibility by 40% within 12 months
2. **Lead Generation** - Generate 500+ qualified leads per month by Q3
3. **Customer Acquisition** - Achieve a customer acquisition cost (CAC) below $50
4. **Retention** - Maintain 80%+ customer retention rate

## Channel Strategy

### 1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

**Objective:** Rank on page 1 for 50+ target keywords within 12 months

- **On-Page SEO:** Optimize all web pages with target keywords, meta descriptions, header tags, and internal linking
- **Technical SEO:** Ensure site speed < 3s, mobile responsiveness, structured data markup, XML sitemaps
- **Content SEO:** Publish 8-12 optimized blog posts per month targeting long-tail keywords
- **Link Building:** Acquire 20+ quality backlinks per month through guest posting, partnerships, and PR

### 2. Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

**Objective:** Achieve 4:1 ROAS on paid search campaigns

- **Google Ads:** Run search, display, and remarketing campaigns
- **Keyword Strategy:** Target high-intent commercial keywords with CPC bidding
- **Ad Copy:** A/B test headlines and descriptions monthly
- **Landing Pages:** Create dedicated landing pages for each campaign with clear CTAs

### 3. Email Marketing

**Objective:** Build a 10,000+ subscriber list with 25%+ open rate

- **List Building:** Lead magnets, gated content, newsletter signups
- **Segmentation:** Segment by behavior, interests, and lifecycle stage
- **Automation:** Welcome series, nurture sequences, re-engagement campaigns
- **Frequency:** 2-3 emails per week (mix of value content and promotional)

### 4. Paid Social Advertising

**Objective:** Generate 200+ leads per month from social ads

- **Platforms:** Facebook/Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads
- **Targeting:** Lookalike audiences, interest-based, retargeting
- **Creative:** Video-first approach with carousel and story formats
- **Budget Split:** 50% Facebook/Instagram, 30% LinkedIn, 20% TikTok

### 5. Influencer Marketing

**Objective:** Partner with 10+ micro-influencers per quarter

- **Selection Criteria:** 10K-100K followers, 3%+ engagement rate, brand alignment
- **Collaboration Types:** Sponsored posts, product reviews, brand ambassadors
- **Tracking:** Unique discount codes and UTM parameters per influencer

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "Digital-First Business Owner"
- **Age:** 28-45
- **Role:** Business owner, marketing manager, or startup founder
- **Pain Points:** Limited marketing budget, lack of digital expertise, need for measurable ROI
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, Instagram, Google Search, YouTube

### Secondary Persona: "Creative Professional"
- **Age:** 22-35
- **Role:** Freelancer, content creator, designer
- **Pain Points:** Building personal brand, finding clients, standing out
- **Channels:** Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, YouTube
